What is a staking pool:

A public stake pool is a Cardano network node with a public address that other users can delegate to, and receive rewards. Ada holders can delegate to public stake pools if they wish to participate in the protocol and receive rewards, but do not wish to operate a Cardano network node themselves.

*Source: cardano.org

  • How to stake tips

    Delegating your stake will be done within your wallet. It will be as easy as selecting a drop-down. You do NOT need to trust a pool, the rewards are distributed by the protocol, not by the pool. You can stake your Ada with hardware wallets and even with paper wallets. Again, no Ada will leave your wallet for staking. Although, It's always adivised to stake using a Hardware device (trezor or ledger), as it's safer, your private keys are never exposed.
